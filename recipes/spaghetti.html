Spaghetti

Go back

Million dollar spaghetti is the perfect combination of noodles, lean ground beef sauce, cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream. It's unbelievably good!

  • 8oz - dy spaghetti
  • 1lb - lean ground beef
  • 16oz - spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 cup - butter, sliced - divided
  • 8oz - cottage cheese
  • 8oz - cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup - sour cream
  • 8oz - shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  1. Step 1 - Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Step 2 - Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 8 to 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Step 3 - While the pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
  4. Step 4 - Transfer to a bowl and mix in spaghetti sauce.
  5. Step 5 - Place 1/2 of the butter slices into the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish. Spread 1/2 of the spaghetti into the dish.
  6. Step 6 - Mix cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream together in a bowl; spread mixture over spaghetti.
  7. Step 7 - Cover with remaining spaghetti and top with remaining slices of butter.
  8. Step 8 - Pour ground beef mixture over spaghetti and spread in an even layer.
  9. Step 9 - Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Spread Cheddar cheese on top and continue baking until cheese has melted and is lightly browned, about 15 minutes more.
