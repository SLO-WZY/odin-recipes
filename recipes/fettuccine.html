This rich and creamy fettuccine Alfredo is an easy recipe even for beginner cooks, but itâ€™s impressive enough for company.

  • 24oz - dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup - butter
  • 3/4 pint - heavy cream
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 dash - garlic salt
  • 3/4 cup - grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup - grated Parmesan cheese
  1. Step 1 - Gather all the indigrients
  2. Step 2 - Cook the pasta for 8 to 10 minutes; drain
  3. Step 3 - Melt butter into cream over low heat, add salt pepper and garlic salt
  4. Step 4 - Increase the heat to medium, stir in grated cheeses until melted
  5. Step 5 - Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated
  6. Step 6 - Enjoy!
